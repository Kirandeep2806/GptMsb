Events

Everything starts here with a Fresher's and sum's up the Farewell

Its the basic of every college to invite the new admissions with the Fresher's party concurrently sending-off the graduated alumni's with the Farewell owing them the respect that they deserve.

Seminars by the significant people

Its a snap where the pupil's attended a seminar concerning the "Recent Advances in the Internet of Things(IoT)" which was delivered by Doctrates and Ph.D alumni's. This is one among them and we still conduct these kind of events consistently inorder to acknowledge the student about the real-time events in their fields...

Step towards the Innovation

Its the step taken by the lecturers of our college to make the students to attend the MHRD(Ministry of Human Resource Development) Director's online seminar for the SIH(Smart India Hackathon) 2020 for bringing up new proposals of the project and making them to represent themselves as well as the college at National Level.

Independence-Day

"The day which we can't even forget, the day which shouldn't be forgotten" i.e., Independence-Day. Events are encouraged, Talents are motivated. Every year we pay tribute to the freedom fighters and owe them all the respect and remember the struggle they have done in the form of Seminars, Elocution, etc.. Everyone are encouraged to participate irrespective of their gender and age.

Technical festivals

We conduct the Tech fests for the people who come with new innovations every year and the winner will be representing himself/ herself at the state level. One such kind of tech fest is "Srujana Tech Fest" which is conducted annually welcoming the new innovations from the various branches...

Sports Day

People who are interested in the games & sports are heartly welcomed and the competition is held in every game/sport. Winner in the college level gets amazing prizes, medals and also they get the opportunity to represent themselves at the District, then the state and finally at the National Level.

Divine heart for the dedicated society

Out of all these we also look after the society following the rules of the government saying "Plant Trees". We took a step ahead to follow the programmes initiated the government i.e., "Haritha Haram" and "Swachh Bharat" inorder to reduce the increasing global warming and pollution. We not only make Engineers but also make responsible citizens.
