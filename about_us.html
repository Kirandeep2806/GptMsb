About College and Us

Government Polytechnic College, Masab Tank which is also known as GPT, Masab Tank is one of the oldest polytechnics in the state, was established in 1923. Originally named as Osmania Central Technical College and located in the Mint Compound at Saifabad. In 1931, it was renamed as Osmania Technical College.
